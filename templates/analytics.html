Digital Twin System Architecture

Understanding the "Software Software Simulation" and AI Model accuracy

1. What is a Digital Twin?

In modern industrial IoT (like environments at GE, Siemens, and Tesla), a Digital Twin is a virtual software representation of a physical piece of hardware.

Because you are viewing this interface without physical hardware (Raspberry Pi, DHT11 Sensors, L298N motors), this entire dashboard acts as a Digital Twin Simulator. The backend Python server replaces the physical microcontroller by mathematically simulating valid telemetry ranges for Temperature, Vibration, and Amperage.

When you use the Hardware Simulation Control Panel, you are instructing the Digital Twin to inject physical faults into the data stream, triggering the AI model exactly as a burnt-out motor would in reality.
